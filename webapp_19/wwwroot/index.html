About Melinda Murdock

Melinda Murdock specializes in supporting individuals, couples, and families facing unique challenges and stressors, particularly within Black communities and marginalized identities. Her extensive training and research in social justice issues and relationship health enrich her therapeutic approach, offering a distinctive perspective. Melinda is committed to equipping clients with new coping mechanisms, reshaping negative behavioral patterns, and transforming relationship dynamics from conflictual cycles to healthier, more empowering connections. Her goal is to empower clients to achieve greater harmony within themselves and in their relationships.
