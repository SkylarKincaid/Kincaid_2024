Blog Post 2

As a licensed marriage and family therapist, I am deeply committed to helping my clients navigate their personal and relational challenges. With a passion for mental health, I have dedicated my career to supporting individuals, couples, and families in their journey toward greater self-awareness and healthier connections.

My expertise lies in assisting clients to process their experiences, enhance their self-awareness, and derive meaning from their situations. I specialize in addressing relationship issues related to conflict, connectivity, and communication, particularly within Black communities and individuals with marginalized identities. My background in social justice issues and relationship health enriches my therapeutic approach, providing a distinctive and impactful perspective.

Outside of my professional life, I am an avid runner who cherishes time with my family. My cat, Puma, holds a special place in my heart and is a source of joy and comfort. Having witnessed the mental health struggles of my own family and friends, I am passionate about making a difference in the lives of others. My goal is to empower my clients with new coping skills, transform negative behaviors and thought patterns, and foster healthier, more empowering relationships.

I look forward to helping you feel more in tune with yourself and others, and to achieve a greater sense of harmony and empowerment in your life.
